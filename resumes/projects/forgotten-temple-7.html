The Forgotten Temple
Summary
Creative group project in creating a fictional story told through video. Our project is based around a female scientist who accidentally travels back in time to when the height of the Khmer Empire.

Skills
¬ Collaboration
¬ Project Planning
¬ Video Editting
¬ Storytelling
 Overview
We created a short film about a scientist exploring the ruins of Angkor Wat. She comes across a trio of radioactive rats, that coincide with an earthquake. No one else feels an earthquake, and suddenly, she’s vanished. By haphazard, she’s transported back in time to the height of the Khmer empire.

To create our short film, we predominantly relied on still images with a few built in-camera effects and live audio voice overs. In camera techniques included camera shake, panning, and zooming. Panning and zooming techniques used are similar to those by documentary film maker Ken Burns.

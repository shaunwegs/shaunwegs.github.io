The Battle of Mag Itha
Summary
A project to create a custom map in the Warcraft game engine based on the First Battle of Ireland, according to Irish mythology. This custom scenario was created in a 3rd party map editing tool for Warcraft: Orcs & Humans.

 Overview
I played Warcraft 2 first, but wanted to start with the original. The sequel has a full map editor; the original only has a unit stats editor. Luckily, someone (BJ Pollard) has reverse engineered how the map files work and created an editor. It took a little work to getting used to the 3rd party tool, but overall it's exactly what I was hoping for.

For my custom map, I wanted to recreate the Battle of Mag Itha, the first battle of Ireland. This story comes from Irish mythology, and in it, the ancient Irish are said to have come from the south and encountered the Fomorians. The Fomorians were a supernatural race seen as agressive and monstrous. They represent darkness, chaos, and death.

I wanted to choose a battle where one side was not human, as I wanted to design a map where you fight the Orcish units. It could be seen as disrespectful to use any battle that was fought between two human armies. I found maps of where it's believed this myth takes place, and used this as the basis for the maps' geography.

In the first of the three maps, you represent Lady Cessair, who led the people to Ireland fleeing the coming flood. Here, you establish a community before heading west. This map is based on an area south of Arklow, County Wicklow, and in the territory of the Déisi.

In the second map, you represent a follower of Partholón, Ith. You travel north through an area close to Dernish Island, County Sligo, a suggested location of the Fomorians island. You intend to clear a plain in order to raise oxen, plough fields, and grow crops. However, you will come across the Fomorians as you move further northward.

The third and final map represents an area along the River Finn, near Lough Swilly in the barony of Raphoe, County Donegal, where the medieval tuath of Magh Itha was. Here, you will battle the Fomorian forces, lead by Cichol Gricenchos "of the withered feet" in a long battle on the Slemna "smooth lands" (plains), which results in the end of the Fomorians.

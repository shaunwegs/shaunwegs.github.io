Cargo Bridge 2

The sequel to the popular construction and physics-based puzzle game. Like the first
game, you're given a limited budget and materials to build bridges to help your workers
transport cargo from one side of a gap to the other, which is tested by the physics engine
of the game. Players use creativity and knowledge to overcome obstacles, inclines, and
increasing difficulty in each level, while managing their limited funds to use
building materials including wood, steel, and rope.

The Magnum Opus Challenge

The Magnum Opus Challenge is an expansion for The Codex of Alchemical Engineering, with fifteen new levels inspired by the winners of a level design contest. If you haven’t beaten The Codex of Alchemical Engineering, you probably shouldn’t play this yet – it’s much harder!

note: I don't know what music originally played in Magnum Opus Challenge. I know Zachtronics liked Evan LE NY's work at this time, so I've used another part of his Rever… Peut-etre… for the page music.

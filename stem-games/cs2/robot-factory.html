Robot Factory

This is an odd little game I made about working in a Robot Factory. Its also about AI takeover and the cost of living crisis. It was my first real attempt at a narrative videogame and I learned a lot from developing it. It takes about 20-25 minutes to complete the game.

I honestly don't have a clue how its going to be received because its admittedly quite depressing. I hope people will give it a go anyway.


Work Quality Assurance at the Robot Factory!

The Robot Factory has recently launched a Robot which armed with intelligent AI, is designed to carry out a variety of tasks from manual labour to customer service. You work as part of the Quality Assurance team whos job it is to ensure that each Robot sold is in perfect shape.
